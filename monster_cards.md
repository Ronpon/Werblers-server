# WERBLERS — MONSTER CARD REFERENCE

This document is the canonical source of truth for all monster card data.
The engine (`content.py`, `effects.py`) must match these specifications.

---

## LEVEL 1 (Tiles 1–30)

### Nose Goblin
- **Flavour:** Should we tell him?
- **Str:** 1
- **Trait:** Calloused. While wearing no Head armour, you have +3 Str.
- **Curse:** Wait, You Lost to THIS? But It Has One Strength! ONE! Just… hang your head in shame…

### Attack Turtle
- **Flavour:** Slow, steady, vicious.
- **Str:** 2
- **Trait:** Hard Shell. You have +2 Str.
- **Curse:** Yer a Hare, Wizard. Pathetic. You lost… to a turtle. Slowpoke. Treat all "6" movement cards as a "1".

### Rake
- **Flavour:** Watch your step!
- **Str:** 2
- **Trait:** Rake It In! When drawing an Equip card, you may discard one currently-equipped card to draw a second new one (level must match current position).
- **Curse:** Eughghghghgh. Snap! If you have more curses than traits, treat all 1 movement cards as a 0. The tile still activates.

### Cursed Teddy Bear
- **Flavour:** So… darn… cute…
- **Str:** 2
- **Trait:** I'm a Grown-up Now! Add a +3 Str. Ted Bearson Minion Card to your Player Board.
- **Curse:** Together Forever. He won't let go! Gain a -2 Str Lonely Teddy Minion card.

### Trouser Snake
- **Flavour:** Surprise!
- **Str:** 3
- **Trait:** BDE. You have +5 Str. when wearing no Footgear.
- **Curse:** It Got In! Discard any equipped Footgear, and any Footgear in your pack.

### Big Rat
- **Flavour:** Unusually large.
- **Str:** 3
- **Trait:** No More Charlie Work! When fighting a monster, you may choose to fight one 1 tier higher than your current tier location.
- **Curse:** Bit o' the Plague. You have -2 Str.

### Slightly Bigger Rat
- **Flavour:** Even less usually sized.
- **Str:** 4
- **Trait:** Rat Smasher. You automatically win any battles against rats or cats.
- **Curse:** Bit More Plague. You have -3 Str.

### Diseased Gnome
- **Flavour:** They're mostly STI's.
- **Str:** 5
- **Trait:** Immunized. Negate the next curse you would receive.
- **Curse:** It's Taking Over! If you are wearing a helmet, put this card under it. It has -3 Str. until you discard it. When you do, discard this card.

### Penis Fly Trap
- **Flavour:** Bonus: +3 Attack vs. Men
- **Str:** 6
- **Trait:** Phallic Dexterity. Your junk is able to protect itself! You are immune to curses that affect footgear.
- **Curse:** Botched Circumcision. Your movement cards have a value of 1 less, with a minimum value of 1.

### Rusty Golem
- **Flavour:** It has seen better days.
- **Str:** 6
- **Trait:** Rust Immunity. You are immune to any cards that cause you to discard weapons.
- **Curse:** The Rust is Spreading! Discard all equipped Weapons.

### Flying Butt
- **Flavour:** Is it cake?
- **Str:** 7
- **Trait:** We All Float Up Here! You may choose not to activate a monster square that you land on.
- **Curse:** Don't look up! You got poop all over your head. If you have a Helm equipped, discard it.

### Gremlin Warrior
- **Flavour:** (no flavour)
- **Str:** 7
- **Trait:** Gremlin's Cunning. You have +2 Str.
- **Curse:** Don't Get It Wet! You have -2 Str.

### Wrecking Ball
- **Flavour:** Please come in!
- **Str:** 8
- **Trait:** Ball and Chain. Take the "Ball and Chain" Item card (+7 Str. 1 handed).
- **Curse:** Get Rekt. Place this card on either your helmet or chest slot. You cannot equip anything there.

### Cat with a Grudge
- **Flavour:** His name is Inigo.
- **Str:** 9
- **Trait:** 8 Lives to Go! You may discard this card to remove one Tier 1 or 2 curse.
- **Curse:** That'll Leave a Mark! You have -3 Str.

## LEVEL 2 (Tiles 31–60)

### Stoned Golem
- **Flavour:** It keeps giggling…
- **Str:** 11
- **Trait:** My Hands are Awesome… Increase your movement card hand size by 1.
- **Curse:** Dude, Where's My Card? You may only hold a maximum of 3 movement cards. Discard if you currently have more than 3.

### Acid Dragon
- **Flavour:** Spittin' acid and trippin' balls.
- **Str:** 13
- **Trait:** I See Everything! When you draw a Monster card, you may put it at the bottom of the deck and draw another.
- **Curse:** Bad Trip. You cannot look at your movement cards. They must be kept facedown and played randomly.

### Sinkhole
- **Flavour:** (no flavour)
- **Str:** 14
- **Trait:** Swiftness. You may run from any combat (except the Werbler) at no cost.
- **Curse:** Quite the Setback. Move back 10 spaces and discard all "3" and "4" movement cards in your hand. Draw as many cards as you discarded.

### Demonic Analrapist
- **Flavour:** He's an analyst AND a therapist!
- **Str:** 14
- **Trait:** Leather Daddy. Add a +1 Str. Token to this card every time you lose a battle.
- **Curse:** Nevernude. You have -5 Str. for every empty Equip slot.

### Misfit Mage
- **Flavour:** Available on Audible!
- **Str:** 15
- **Trait:** It got better! Remove one curse from your character, then discard this card.
- **Curse:** Turned Into a Newt! You got better, but not ALL better. You have -3 Str.

### Blood Golem
- **Flavour:** (no flavour)
- **Str:** 15
- **Trait:** Blood Pact. You have +3 Str.
- **Curse:** Blood Drain. You have -4 Str.

### Smelly Cat
- **Flavour:** What are they feeding it?
- **Str:** 16
- **Trait:** It's Not Your Fault! If you lose a battle, you may discard this card to take that Monster's Trait instead of its Curse.
- **Curse:** The Smell Won't Come Out! Discard 2 Equip cards of your choice from your Player Board.

### Twisted Treant
- **Flavour:** (no flavour)
- **Str:** 17
- **Trait:** Tough Skin. When you have no Chest armour, gain +10 Str.
- **Curse:** They Flooded Your Base! Discard all Items in your pack.

### Bogeyman
- **Flavour:** Time to boogie.
- **Str:** 18
- **Trait:** You Got a Birdie! Take a Power Driver Equip Card (2h, +10 Str).
- **Curse:** Scared of the Dark. At night, all your movement cards have -1 movement.

### Wood Golem
- **Flavour:** Stronger in the morning.
- **Str:** 18
- **Trait:** Bark Worse Than its Bite. For every empty Equip slot, you have +3 Str.
- **Curse:** Termites! You have -5 Str.

### Coronavirus
- **Flavour:** Guess when I started working on this game!
- **Str:** 19
- **Trait:** Vaxxed! You cannot take on any new Tier 2 curses.
- **Curse:** Facial Coverings Required. If you have no helm, you have -10 Str.

### Bad Mexican Food
- **Flavour:** Even spicier on its way out.
- **Str:** 19
- **Trait:** Stomach of Steel. You have +3 Str.
- **Curse:** I Need a Place to Go! If you're wearing boots, discard them.

### Fat Troll
- **Flavour:** Or was it "fat roll"?
- **Str:** 20
- **Trait:** Big Boned! You are so large, you can have 2 Chest armours equipped at once.
- **Curse:** So… Lethargic… Whenever you use a 3 or a 4, add a -1 Str. token to this card.

## LEVEL 3 (Tiles 61–90)

### One of Those Fish That Swim Up Your Urethra
- **Flavour:** Ew.
- **Str:** 21
- **Trait:** Strong Schlong. Add five +1 Str. tokens to this card. When your opponent enters battle, you may discard one or more to give them -3 Str.
- **Curse:** It's Wriggling! Whenever you take on a new curse, discard a trait of your choice.

### Zombie Linebacker
- **Flavour:** (TBD)
- **Str:** 22
- **Trait:** Touchdown! +3 Str. You may discard this card to travel directly to the Werbler square.
- **Curse:** Roughing the Kicker! Move back 15 spaces.

### Large Orc
- **Flavour:** (TBD)
- **Str:** 23
- **Trait:** Meat's Back On the Menu! You may force one opponent to discard a minion card of your choice. When you do this, add five +1 Str. tokens to this card.
- **Curse:** You're On the Menu… Put a -2 Str. "Bloody Stump" card on either an arm slot or your boot slot. Discard any card it replaces.

### Spooky Ghost
- **Flavour:** It's 3 spooky 5 me.
- **Str:** 23
- **Trait:** Phase Shift. Before you move, you may discard 1 Equip card to change time of day.
- **Curse:** Out of Phase. Your pack can only carry 1 item instead of 3.

### Wicked Witch
- **Flavour:** Hotter than they used to be.
- **Str:** 23
- **Trait:** She's Melting! Take a "Vial of Liquid Witch" card. You may discard it to give a monster or player -10 Str. for one battle.
- **Curse:** Cursed! The next time you face a monster (other than the Werbler) discard this card. You lose that battle.

### Swamp Monster
- **Flavour:** (TBD)
- **Str:** 24
- **Trait:** He's Just Misunderstood! Gain a +7 Str. Swamp Friend Minion card.
- **Curse:** Laundry Day! Remove all armour from chest, head, or feet. If you have no room in your pack, discard them.

### Creepy Hollywood Exec
- **Flavour:** Bonus: If Roofie Demon has been defeated, you win this battle.
- **Str:** 25
- **Trait:** Me Too! Whenever an opponent discards a curse, you may discard one of your own.
- **Curse:** Blacklisted. Go back to "Start".

### Necrodancer
- **Flavour:** Busting moves and raising dudes.
- **Str:** 25
- **Trait:** Fancy Footwork. When playing a movement card, you may give it -1 or -2 if you wish.
- **Curse:** Can't Stop the Music! Replace your Footgear with -5 Str. "Dancing Shoes" and discard any card it replaces.

### Spanish Inquisition
- **Flavour:** Didn't expect that!
- **Str:** 25
- **Trait:** I'll Come In Again. When drawing a Monster card, you may place it on the bottom of the monster deck and draw another.
- **Curse:** Now, Cardinal, the Rack! Discard 1 Trait or Equip card for every curse afflicting you.

### Goblin Warrior
- **Flavour:** (TBD)
- **Str:** 26
- **Trait:** Scavenger. When you draw an Equip card, you may put it on the bottom of the deck and draw another.
- **Curse:** Stabbed. Add one -1 Str. Token to this card for every curse afflicting you. If a curse is lifted, remove a token.

### Guy With a Gun
- **Flavour:** Unoriginal, but still dangerous.
- **Str:** 26
- **Trait:** Kapwing! Take a +8 Str. "Bulletproof Vest" card.
- **Curse:** Shot Through the Heart. A Gunshot Wound card takes the place of your Chest Armour slot. Discard any Chest Armour it replaces.

### Skeletal Overlord
- **Flavour:** (TBD)
- **Str:** 27
- **Trait:** I'm De Overlord Now. Take a +3 Str. "Minion Wrangler" card, which gives your other minions +2 Str. each.
- **Curse:** Enslaved. Give 2 Equip cards (from your pack or your player board) to an opponent, if they have room. Discard any cards they don't have room for (you choose which to discard).

### Demon Lord
- **Flavour:** (TBD)
- **Str:** 27
- **Trait:** New Lord in Town. Gain a +8 Str. "Demon Spawn" Minion card.
- **Curse:** KNEEL! Your Werbler has +10 Str. the next time you fight it. Discard this card if you lose.

### Velociraptor
- **Flavour:** Where's Chris Pratt when you need him?
- **Str:** 28
- **Trait:** You're the Alpha! Gain a +7 Str. Pet Velociraptor Minion card.
- **Curse:** Clever Girl. Discard all items in your pack.

### Roofie Demon
- **Flavour:** Bonus: If Creepy Hollywood Exec has been defeated, you win this battle.
- **Str:** 29
- **Trait:** Residuals. Every turn, add a +1 Str. token to this card.
- **Curse:** My Drink Tastes Funny… Discard 2 currently equipped cards.

### Jeffrey Dahmer as a Vampire
- **Flavour:** Kinda overkill, right?
- **Str:** 30
- **Trait:** Strengthened by Taint. You have +2 Str. for every curse afflicting you.
- **Curse:** He Drank Your Blood. Then Ate Your Arm. Replace your right arm Equip slot with a -3 Str. Bloody Stump, discarding anything it replaces.
